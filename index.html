About 420 on Green Street

420 on Green Street started out as an attempt to provide the marijuana enthusiast with access to a wide array of Marijuana strains but has now evolved to provide the Culture with a "Private Growing Space" that they can rent out monthly fully maintained by our experts at reasonable rates. They can then choose to use their harvest for personal use (as per legislation) or to *sell to our network of enthusiasts for medicinal purposes.

*reseller option only.
